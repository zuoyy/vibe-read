# Design Document: The Three-Body Problem (Trilogy)

## 1. Vibe & Philosophy
**Keywords**: Cosmic, Chaotic, Cold, Mathematical, Monumental.
**Visual Metaphor**: The Three Suns, The Droplet, The Vector Foil.
**Design System**: **Unified Optical System (统一视幻系统)**
- **All Volumes** share the same "Deep Space Black" background.
- differentiation comes from **Accent Colors** and **Key Visuals**, not different textures.

## 2. Color Palette (Unified)
- **Background**: Deep Space Black `#050505` ( All Pages )
- **Text**: Orbital White `#FFFFFF`
- **Vol 1 Accent**: Warning Red `#FF3333` (The Signal)
- **Vol 2 Accent**: Forest Green/Teal `#00FFAA` (The Dark Forest / The Drop) -> *Actually, let's keep it colder. Cyan/Ice Blue.*
- **Vol 3 Accent**: Dimension Purple `#BF00FF` (2D Vector Foil) -> *Or Pure White for "Death".*

## 3. Structure
- **Hub Page**: A "Trilogy Dashboard".
- **Volume Pages**: Consistent "Hero" with big typography and specific immersive visual per volume.
